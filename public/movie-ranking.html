The Best Movies According to Rahimah

My top 3 movies of all times

Dangal

It told the story of how Mahavir Singh Phogat, an amateur pehalwan trained his daughters, Geeta and Babita, to become gold medal-winning wrestlers.

3 idiots

Narrated through two parallel dramas, one in the present and the other set ten years in the past, the story follows the friendship of three students at an Indian engineering college and is a satire about the social pressures under the Indian education system.

Taare zameen par

Ishaan is criticised by his parents for his poor academic performance and is sent away to a boarding school. Ram, an art teacher, however, realises he has dyslexia and helps him uncover his potential.
